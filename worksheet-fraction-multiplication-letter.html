    Worksheet Worksheet: Fraction Multiplication

    Objectives

    • Multiply fractions by whole numbers.
    • Multiply fractions by other fractions.
    • Divide fractions using reciprocals.
    • Begin simplifying fractions.

    Multiply each pair of numbers. Can you write the product as a whole number?
